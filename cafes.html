The Green Housekeeper

The Green Housekeeper Cafe is a small friendly café serving home cooked food made from local produce. Their menu has been simplified; we use the same quality ingredients as always just not quite so many variants. Do remember that if there is something that you would like that is not listed here please just ask a member of the team. If we can make it, we will make it. All food is served all day 10am – 4pm. Enjoy!

  1. https://greenhousek.co.uk/
  2. PRICE RANGE £2 - £7
  3. SPECIAL DIETS - Vegetarian Friendly, Vegan Options, Gluten Free

Fresher's Café

Famously friendly café in Ambleside, the heart of the English Lake District. Delicious homemade food including all-day breakfast, spectacular quiches, sandwiches, toasties and jackets, fresh homemade soups and cakes galore. Vegan and GF options. Premium coffees, selection of teas, smoothies, milkshakes, ice creams and iced coffees. Accessible by wheelchair users All food is served all day 9am – 3pm. Enjoy!

  1. https://fresherscafe.co.uk/
  2. PRICE RANGE £2 - £7
  3. SPECIAL DIETS - Vegetarian Friendly, Vegan Options, Gluten Free

Copper Pot Cafe

The little café in the heart of the English Lake District. A cosy hub in the centre of Ambleside, crammed full of delicious home cooked food and cakes, fantastic roasted coffee, and great music! Accessible by wheelchair users - please call for further information. Child friendly, dog friendly, bike-friendly, basically all-round friendly family run business. We can cater for specific dietary requirements, allergies and intolerances. We are aware of the ingredients used in all of our dishes. All food is served all day 10am – 4pm. Enjoy!(Closed Tuesday - Wednesday)

  1. https://www.copper-pot.co.uk/
  2. PRICE RANGE £2 - £7
  3. SPECIAL DIETS - Vegetarian Friendly, Vegan Options, Gluten Free
