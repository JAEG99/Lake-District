Premier Inn Keswick Hotel

Whether you’re planning romantic breaks in the Lake District or activity weekends with the kids, our cosy Premier Inn Keswick hotel is the perfect base for getting the most out your stay. This beautiful region is home to some of the best scenery in the UK, so holidays in Cumbria wouldn’t be complete without getting out and exploring the great outdoors! We’re just a 15-minute drive away from the famous Lake District National Park, a must-visit whether you’re cycling around the lakes or hiking up Skiddaw. Friars Crag and Derwentwater are nearby too, and little ones will love visiting quirky spots like the Derwent Pencil Museum. After fun days out in Cumbria, you can look forward to a relaxing evening back at our great-value hotel and a cosy night’s sleep on a super-comfy bed.
